Ray Tracing Graphics


Ray tracing draws an image by emulating the way light behaves in the real world. It traces the path of simulated light rays and the software calculates the ways that light will reflect, refract or scatter when it encounters an object. [8] This allows the artist to create highly realistic images and can provide a close approximation of a three dimensional image.
